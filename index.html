Technical Specifications & Projects

Cloning Feasibility Model (CFM) v0.40

A stochastic risk engine utilizing Monte Carlo methods (2,000 draws/run) to quantify mammalian cloning success.

  • Success Metric: Validated at a 0.57% mean success rate, aligning with historical primate SCNT benchmarks.
  • Uncertainty Analysis: Produces P05 / P50 / P95 bands to model biological variance.
  • Bottleneck Detection: Automated sensitivity correlation identifies placenta and embryo cleavage as primary failure gates.

Bio-Simulation Engine (BSE)

Mechanistic PK/PD multi-organ simulator designed for AI-assisted ADMET research and virtual physiology modeling.

  • Architecture: Compartmental dynamical systems with linked fluid/tox flows.
  • Objective: High-fidelity simulation of drug/stressor responses to bypass traditional wet-lab friction.

Neural Self-Consistent System (NSCS)

Recursive optimization framework designed for symbolic regression and scientific equation discovery.

  • Discovery Logic: Iterative candidate generation with neural residual correction for long-term model stability.
