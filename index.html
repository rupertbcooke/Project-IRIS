Technical Specifications

ACTIVE SIMULATOR

Cloning Feasibility Model (CFM) v0.40

A stochastic risk assessment engine for mammalian cloning via SCNT, utilizing uncertainty quantification and sensitivity analysis.

  • Methodology: Monte Carlo simulation (2,000 draws/run) producing P05/P50/P95 bands.
  • Sensitivity Layer: Automated gate–outcome correlation to identify bottlenecks, such as placenta vs. embryo cleavage.
  • Current Metric: 0.57% mean success probability, validated against historical SCNT data.
  • Reproducibility: Dynamic evidence ingestion via local evidence.csv with machine-readable run logs.
VIRTUAL PHYSIOLOGY

Bio-Simulation Engine (BSE)

A modular engine for simulating physiological responses to drugs, immune triggers, and stressors.

  • Modeling Approach: Mechanistic multi-organ PK/PD with linked compartment flows and ADME.
  • Capabilities: Adjustable physiological parameters, toxicity thresholds, and live visualization of compartment states.
  • Scaling: Preliminary cross-species scaling (Rabbit → Human) currently under execution.
DISCOVERY ENGINE

Neural Self-Consistent System (NSCS)

An AI-assisted framework for iterative equation discovery and refinement of mechanistic simulators.

  • Methodology: Recursive symbolic regression followed by neural residual correction where mechanistic forms underfit.
  • Optimization: Evaluates and rejects unstable candidate equations to enforce long-term simulator stability.
